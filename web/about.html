Product Overview

Flock

Digital queue management for restaurants

Flock helps high-volume F&B venues in India manage guest waitlists digitally. Guests join a queue by scanning a QR code at the venue, track their wait time in real time, and receive a WhatsApp notification when their table is ready.

How it works

  1. Guest scans a QR code at the venue and enters their name, phone number, and party size.
  2. Guest sees their queue position and estimated wait time on a live status page.
  3. When their table is ready, staff notifies them and the guest receives a WhatsApp message instantly.

WhatsApp use case

Flock sends two transactional, guest-initiated WhatsApp messages once a guest has entered the waitlist and provided explicit consent.

Join confirmation Sent when a guest joins the waitlist. Includes their queue position and a link to their live status page.
Table-ready notification Sent when staff marks their table as ready. Prompts the guest to return to the host desk within 3 minutes.

Current pilot venue

Flock is currently being piloted at The Craftery by Subko, Koramangala, Bengaluru, a high-volume specialty coffee and dining venue.
